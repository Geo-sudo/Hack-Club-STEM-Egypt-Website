CYBERQUEST

Rules & Guidelines

  • Each team must have at LEAST one laptop
  • Each team must have a stable internet connection
  • Text between () or {} can be either a solution for the clue or a piece of information that you might need in other clue
  • Each clue has a unique solution, you will need to enter it in the input field to proceed to the next clue
  • The syntax of the solution is capatalized words with underscores between each word not spaces, such as I_Love_Hack_Club, unless you are given the solution, at that time you have to copy and paste it in the input field
  • clues without a text field indicates that you have to go to a specific place to scan a QR code
  • Use your imagination extensively
  • Have FUN
