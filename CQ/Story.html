CYBERQUEST

You’re the Draft 0xNN, a well-renowned division of the Hack Club’s galactic army. You’re traveling near the borders of the human galactic expansion, the edges of the Hackers' Haven galaxy. It’s a cold and empty area, even for the abyss that is space. However empty the void may seem, something lurks in the shadows. The edges of Hackers' Haven, being deserted of any human activity other than the occasional hydrogen mining, is a dangerous place filled with rogue bandits, namely the criminal organization Data Raiders. You’ve been informed by General Luminar that a raiders’ ship is nearing a local hydrogen mine in a gas giant in the Hydrogeniae Prime’s planetary system. Your mission assigned is simple. Lead your forces into the Data Raiders’ ship magnetosphere without being noticed, infiltrate and navigate the ship with power, and finally seize control of the ship and put an end to these data snatchers.
